What is Kanvas?

Bored of sitting at home during quarantine? Tired of being cooped up and not being able to go out? We bring to you the perfect way to use this spare time productively. GAWDS presents to you, Kanvas'20, a platform for all you web and graphics geeks to showcase your talents. Create a Beautiful Graphic or build an Awwwsome Website to win a prize of 1.5K. So let the gears in your brain churn and your creative juices flow freely, and come up with something that will blow our minds!
