THEME : CREATE A DASHBOARD TO TRACK AND DISPLAY DATA ABOUT COVID-19


Rules and regulations:

1.The contest is open for both, students of NIT Kurukshetra and students of other colleges/universities.
2.Please submit your entries within the given timeline.
3.The content of the submission should be clearly based on the given theme, otherwise points may be deducted.
4.Plagiarism of any type will not be tolerated and will lead to immediate disqualification.
